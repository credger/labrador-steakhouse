ABOUT US

Labrador Steakhouse is first-rate steakhouse dedicated to offering perfectly cooked steak and a great atmosphere. We serve 100% grass-fed beef sourced from local farms. Our drink offerings include over 20 beers on tap, an extensive cocktail menu, and wines from some of the best vinyards around the world.

We spent months working with interior designers to craft the perfect feel for our steakhouse. Inspired by modern mountain cabin architecture, our dining space features an ensemble of beautiful timber beams, decorative stone, and elaborate fireplaces. We hope you enjoy your dining experience as much as we did creating it!

-Travis & Mattie Woods
 Owners, Labrador Steakhouse
